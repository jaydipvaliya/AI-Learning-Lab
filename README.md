## About

Hi, I'm **Jaydip**.

I started this repository to document my journey of becoming an AI Engineer.

Instead of rushing through tutorials, I want to build a deep understanding of Artificial Intelligence—from Python fundamentals to Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Large Language Models, AI Infrastructure, and eventually AI Research.

This repository is my personal knowledge base where I document everything I learn, build, and experiment with.

---

## Why This Repository Exists

My goal is **not only to use AI**.

I want to understand:

- How modern AI systems work
- How Large Language Models are trained
- How AI infrastructure is built
- Why problems like hallucinations and model collapse happen
- How future AI systems can become more reliable

Ultimately, I hope to contribute to solving real AI challenges rather than simply building applications on top of existing models.

---

## My Long-Term Goal

Become an AI Systems Engineer capable of:

- Building production-ready AI applications
- Understanding LLM architecture
- Reading and implementing research papers
- Contributing to open-source AI projects
- Building AI products and startups
- Conducting research on future AI challenges
